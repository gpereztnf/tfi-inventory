Formula Definitions — Executive Summary
Total Inventory CostΣ(Lot Cost) across all lots in the WMS snapshot. Lot Cost = On-Hand Qty × Actual Unit Cost as recorded at receipt.
Aged 90+ DaysSum of Lot Cost where Days On Hand ≥ 90. Days On Hand = Report Date − Receive Date. AT SEA lots included in total but flagged separately.
Aged 150+ DaysSum of Lot Cost where Days On Hand ≥ 150. The oldest aged tier — flags items that have been on hand the longest and require disposition attention.
Expiring Within 90 DaysSum of Lot Cost where Expiration Date − Report Date ≤ 90. These lots need to be sold or actioned before their expiration date.
Portfolio DIOCost-weighted average days on hand. DIO = Σ(Lot Cost × Days On Hand) ÷ Σ(Lot Cost). Excludes AT SEA. No sales data required.

Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) measures how many days of cost are tied up in stock. Calculated as the cost-weighted average days on hand across all lots — no sales data required. DIO = Σ(Lot Cost × Days On Hand) ÷ Σ(Lot Cost). Lower DIO = faster-turning inventory. Higher DIO = dollars sitting longer before moving.

Formula Definitions — DIO Analysis
Portfolio DIOΣ(Lot Cost × Days On Hand) ÷ Σ(Lot Cost). Cost-weighted average. A lot worth $100K sitting 90 days pulls DIO up 9,000 day-dollars; a lot worth $1K has negligible effect. Excludes AT SEA in-transit stock.
DIO — 90+ Day LotsSame formula, restricted to lots in the 90–119, 120–149, and 150+ buckets. Represents the average age of your working capital drag tier.
DIO — 150+ Day LotsSame formula, restricted to lots aged ≥ 150 days. Tells you the typical age of your most distressed inventory — not just that it's old, but how old on average.
Dead-Stock CandidatesCount of distinct items where item-level DIO ≥ 150 days. Calculated per item as Σ(Lot Cost × Days On Hand) ÷ Σ(Lot Cost) across all lots for that item.
DIO Trend (proxy)When loaded from monthly snapshots, DIO is approximated using bucket midpoints: Σ(Bucket Midpoint × Bucket Cost) ÷ Total Cost. Midpoints: ≤30→15, 30–59→45, 60–89→75, 90–119→105, 120–149→135, 150+→180. Exact DIO activates with live DB.
